Who Are We

We are a Pasadena, California–based team with academic and industrial expertise in lithium-ion batteries. We have developed a 100% silicon anode that significantly improves battery energy density.

Our Element100 Anode is manufactured with flexible customization options to meet your specific performance and development requirements. Beyond supplying electrodes, we also act as your technology partner, by supporting you from concept and cell development through pilot production and scale-up.

Our Services

1

Supply of Element100 anodes

We manufacture our 100% silicon anode using a simple, water-based, and cost-efficient state-of-the-art electrode fabrication process. Contact us with your technical requirements, and we will tailor the anode accordingly.

2

Battery performance testing

We assess your cathode with our Element100 anode performance, providing interactive prototyping with performance feedback such as energy density and cycle life. Element100 Technology is not just a supplier, but also a technology partner.

3

Collaboration

We partner with battery companies from concept and cell development through pilot production and scale-up, bringing our R&D and manufacturing expertise to accelerate performance and industrialization.
